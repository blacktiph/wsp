Site Intent

The redesign of the website for BlacktipH Fishing is to reorganize the content, create a more dynamic and usable design, increase web traffic, and to create an overall professional experience for the visitors. The website lacks design and creativity.

The client doesn’t update their fishing reports page, so it would be best to eliminate them altogether. The “shark fishing” page and the “tips & techniques” page are mostly embedded videos and can be eliminated until the client has more content to place on these pages. The YouTube tab on the navigation can be eliminated and replaced with a YouTube icon. The links page is not relevant to the show or their sponsors and therefore can be eliminated. In the “about us” dropdown menu, “our partners” doesn’t have to be it’s own page, they can put in a section on the home page. The “in the media” does not need to be in a dropdown menu, rather it can be it’s own tab. Therefore the total pages of the website are reduced to six (6): Home, “About Us”, “Image Gallery”, Press, FAQ, and “Contact Us”. The logo will be in the top left along with a horizontal navigation menu. We will create a clean banner that is well branded.

Competitive Analysis

Main Competitors

  • Addictive Fishing
  • Facts of Fishing
  • Fishing the Flats

The main competition have more clean and usable websites. Their websites offer more ways for their fans to interact with them. The competitors have a larger budget than BlacktipH Fishing. Visitors are spending more time on the competition websites.

Bad Practices

  • The website for "Addictive Fishing" is too busy as you get near the bottom. The eye is overwhelmed in making a selection between the options available.
  • The website for Facts of Fishing lacks interactive design. There are not hover effects on the navigation or any indication informing the user which page they are on. The website is very static.
  • The website for Fishing the Flats is very busy. The use of white spaced is at a minimum. There are two many options in the sidebar, the user quickly becomes overwhelmed by the many choices available in a relatively small space.

Targeted Audience

Based on our research, our targeted audience are males between the ages of 13 and 65. Users that visit the website are using Internet Explorer, Chrome, Safari, and Firefox browsers, therefore the website will have to work in all these browsers. In terms of mobile platforms, the website must have media queries for all Apple platforms (Iphone and Ipad) and Android platforms. We will have to create a more user centered design to bring users back to the website.
